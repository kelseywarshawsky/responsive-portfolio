About Me

My pursuit of knowledge and expanded skill set has been a passion for me, leading me to push into new challenges and growth opportunities. If you have a project that seems like a challenge, I'm hoping to blow past your expectations.

In my spare time you'll find me painting (or trying to learn), cooking, and adopting too many cats. I really enjoy a great book, sampling the latest craft beers, or spending time with my family. I love to spend my summers camping with my dad or on my family's farm with my niece and nephews.
